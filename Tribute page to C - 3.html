☛ Public Engagement: The mission engages the public through its scientific endeavors, fostering curiosity and interest in space exploration among people of all ages.

☛ Technological Spin-offs: The technologies developed for Chandrayaan-3 might find applications beyond space exploration, benefiting various industries on Earth.

☛ Boosting Space Tourism: The mission's success could spark interest in lunar tourism, paving the way for private ventures and commercial space travel.
